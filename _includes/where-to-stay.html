Where To Stay

We have a hotel block at the Trailside Inn. Dave's stayed there for the Wistia team ski trip in the past, and it's awesome. The innkeepers Josh and Ann are super friendly, and the inn is conveniently located just down the street from the venue. Transportation will be provided back and forth, too.

To book a room at Trailside, call Josh and Ann at (802) 422-8800. Don't try to book on the website – it's blocked for those dates, for you!

If you're feeling a little fancier (not that Trailside isn't fancy), check out the Killington Grand Resort Hotel. Call 1-800-324-6819 to get in on the block rate.
